封装的angular版datetime-picker

from: http://plnkr.co/edit/jZ6QyrLNajRgIMSlUqsg?p=preview

formated：格式化值

ng-model: abc1={{abc1}}

jsTimeStamp：js时间戳，异步恢复编辑

ng-model: abc2={{abc2}}

phpTimeStamp：php时间戳

ng-model: abc3={{abc3}} 
<datetime-picker 
    ng-model="abc1" 
    data-date-format="yyyy-mm-dd hh:ii:00" 
    data-show-meridian="false" 
    data-return-type="formated" 
    class="input-group date">
</datetime-picker>

class="input-group date" 中 date 为必须项

data-return-type 取值为：phpTimeStamp、jsTimeStamp、formated

其它属性设置，直接添加在标签上
